Bolognese

Description

A classic Bolognese Sauce should be in every home cook’s repertoire! My version is smooth, rich, hearty and slow cooked to perfection. The very essence of Italian comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 400g/14oz beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 100g/3½oz carrot, grated

Steps

  1. Heat a large saucepan over a medium heat.
  2. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and once hot add the beef mince and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Beat the 3 large eggs in a medium bowl and season with a little freshly grated black pepper. Set everything aside.
